Glimmerfall TCG

Quick Rules & Keyword Glossary

Card Types

  • Entity: Characters or creatures that are deployed to the battlefield. They have Power (damage dealt) and Health (damage they can take), and can attack the opponent or defend your Nexus.
  • Rite: A slow spell that has a one-time effect and is then placed in the discard pile. Can only be played during your main phase.
  • Flash: A fast spell that has a one-time effect and is then placed in the discard pile. Can be played at any time, even during combat or on your opponent's turn.
  • Artifact: Items or structures that stay on the battlefield and provide ongoing passive bonuses or activated abilities.

Keywords Glossary

Some cards have special abilities that are represented by single words (Keywords). Here is what they mean:

  • Guard: This Entity protects you. Opposing Entities must attack an Entity with Guard before they can attack your Nexus or other Entities.
  • Evasive: This Entity is hard to catch. It can only be blocked by other Entities that also have Evasive.
  • Stealth: This Entity cannot be targeted by enemy Rites or attacked by enemy Entities until it attacks for the first time.
  • Lethal: Any amount of combat damage dealt by this Entity is enough to instantly destroy the opposing Entity, regardless of its remaining Health.
  • Overwhelm: When this Entity attacks and destroys a blocking Entity, any excess damage is dealt directly to the opponent's Nexus.

Basic Gameplay Loop

1. Ready Phase: Ready all your exhausted (tapped) cards and gain your maximum Energy/Mana for the turn.

2. Draw Phase: Draw 1 card from your deck.

3. Main Phase: Spend Energy to deploy Entities, Rites, and Artifacts. You may also declare attacks with your ready Entities.

4. End Phase: Pass the turn to your opponent.

Victory Condition: Reduce your opponent's Nexus Health to 0!
